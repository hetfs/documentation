### **Markdown Editors**

[Markdown](https://www.markdownguide.org/) has become the go-to format for technical documentation due to its simplicity, flexibility, and efficiency.

- **Features**: Lightweight text editors that use Markdown syntax to simplify formatting. They offer a distraction-free writing environment and effortless conversion to various formats.
- **Ideal For**: Writers seeking a streamlined approach to create clean, well-structured documentation.
